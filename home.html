Welcome to University Basketball Club

Experience the thrill of basketball with our dedicated team. Join us in celebrating the spirit of the game! For the first time in history, BFI is committed to shaping the future of basketball and structuring the game for the next generation to embrace as a sport. BFI is pleased to announce our first collaboration with Manipal University for the National Basketball Academy. This marks a significant step in laying the groundwork for grassroots development. With many more initiatives in the pipeline, we are delighted to see like-minded individuals joining hands to foster the growth of basketball across the nation.
